LASAGNA RECIPE

lasagna dish

This is lasagna made of miced meat and
dough

Ingredients...

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion and garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes or spaghetti sauce
  • Cottage cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese

Steps...

  1. In a small bowl, combine the first four ingredients; spread on four slices of bread. Layer with bacon, tomato and cheese; top with remaining bread.
  2. In a large skillet or griddle, melt 2 tablespoons butter. Toast sandwiches until lightly browned on both sides and cheese is melted, adding butter if necessary.
  3. Nutrition Facts 1 sandwich: 445 calories, 24g fat (12g saturated fat), 66mg cholesterol, 1094mg sodium, 35g carbohydrate (3g sugars, 2g fiber), 21g protein.
